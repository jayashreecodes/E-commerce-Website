About Us:



vinayagar South Indian Kitchen

Welcome to vinayagar South Indian Kitchen, where tradition and flavor come together to create an unforgettable dining experience. Located in the heart of the city, our restaurant is dedicated to bringing you the authentic tastes of South India.

Our Story

At vinayagar South Indian Kitchen, our journey began with a passion for authentic South Indian cuisine. Our founders, hailing from the vibrant states of Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, and Kerala, envisioned a place where the rich culinary heritage of South India could be shared with everyone. We strive to honor these traditions with every dish we serve.

Our Cuisine

South Indian cuisine is renowned for its diverse flavors and ingredients. Our menu showcases a variety of dishes from dosas and idlis to sambar and biryanis. We pride ourselves on using traditional recipes and the freshest ingredients, from fragrant spices to locally sourced vegetables.

Signature Dishes



Masala Dosa: A crispy, golden crepe filled with a spiced potato mixture, served with coconut chutney and sambar. Chicken Chettinad: A spicy, aromatic chicken curry featuring a blend of roasted spices. Vegetable Biryani: Fragrant basmati rice cooked with a medley of vegetables and aromatic spices. Desserts & Beverages



End your meal with our delectable South Indian sweets like Rava Kesari and Payasam. Pair your food with traditional beverages such as Filter Coffee or Buttermilk.

Our Ambiance



vinayagar South Indian Kitchen offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, perfect for family gatherings, casual dining, or special occasions. Our decor reflects the vibrant culture of South India, creating a truly immersive dining experience.
